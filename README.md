# E-Commerce-KPI-Dashboard
Through this Project i analyzed 1,000+ sales records across 7 regions, 4 brands, 4 sales channels, and 5 industries to derive actionable business insights. Built an interactive Excel dashboard with multiple KPIs, including Total Sales (₹5.45L+), Region-wise Performance, Brand Contribution, and Channel Analysis.

Identified top-performing brand contributing ~26% of total revenue, enabling focused marketing and inventory decisions.

Conducted region-wise analysis across 7 global markets, identifying key revenue-driving regions contributing up to 15% of total sales each.

Designed dynamic dashboards using Pivot Tables, Charts, and Slicers, allowing real-time filtering by Year, Month, Region, Brand, and Channel.

Analyzed sales channel performance (Online, Partner, Telesales, etc.) to evaluate revenue distribution and optimize channel strategy.

Developed separate analytical views for Sales Rep, Brand, Industry, and Channel performance, improving decision-making efficiency.

Automated reporting and reduced manual analysis effort by 70–80% through structured Excel dashboards.

Tracked multi-year sales trends (3 years of data) to identify growth patterns and seasonal demand.
